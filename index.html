Key Projects

Here are some highlights of the projects I have worked on:

Star - Galaxy - QSO Image Classification

I am currently working under Prof. Ajit Kembhavi (IUCAA, Pune). We are exploring the use of variational autoencoders and convolutional networks for the image classification of stars, quasars and galaxies having small angular sizes from the SDSS DR16 dataset.

Time-Series Classification of LSST Photometric Data

My teammate and I worked on developing a solution for the PLAsTiCC challenge. Our Stacked ensemble of GRU and Dense networks was trained on 7878 samples and achieved an accuracy of 76.2% on a test set consisting of over 2.5 million samples.

Thermodynamic Properties of Ice

My team and I implemented a Monte Carlo algorithm to study the thermodynamic properties of ice. We computed the residual entropy and identified a phase transition.

Authorship Identification

My teammate and I implemented an algorithm to identify the author of an unknown text, by comparing the relative n-gram frequencies for each author, similar to K-nearest neigbours.
